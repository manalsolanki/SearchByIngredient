Macrooni

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon corn starch or corn flour or ½ to 1 potato finely chopped
  • ¼ cup green peas
  • ¼ cup carrots finely chopped
  • 4 to 6 beans finely chopped
  • 2 to 3 spring onions chopped or red onions
  • 2 ½ to 3 cups water or stock

Directions

  1. Chop carrots, beans and spring onions.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and saute ginger garlic until aromatic.
  3. Next add spring onion whites, carrots, beans,peas & sweet corn kernels.
  4. Saute for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Pour water or stock. Boil till all the veggies get cooked.
  6. Cooking on a low flame makes the soup taste better.
  7. Optional for restaurant style - While the soup boils, mix 1 tsp corn starch with 3 to 4tbsp water.
  8. When the veggies are soft cooked, pour this slowly to the pot and stir till it thickens.
  9. Add salt and sugar as well. Taste and add more salt if needed.
  10. Add the spring onion greens and pepper.
  11. Transfer sweet corn soup to bowls.
  12. If desired can add some vinegar, green chilli sauce and ground white pepper.
